LLM-based Generator of Synthetic Personas

Personas help designers understand the needs, goals, and behaviors of their target users. By providing a brief description of the product, this tool can tailor the personas to reflect the specific context and use cases of the product, ensuring that the personas are relevant and useful for guiding the design process. Example: "I am designing an application to manage university orientation for students about to graduate, so that they can choose the university path that best suits them."

The LLM will create your persona will by following instructions/guildelines provided in this field. You can specify elements that are expected or put some constraints. Example: "Set an appropriate name, age and personality" or "Name is John, generate age and personality"
